Korm bip

Purangs korm bip is salt-cured brisket of beef. The term comes from the treatment of the meat with large-grained rock salt, also called "corns" of salt.

ORDER KORM BIP
Kare-Kare

Purangs Kare-Kare is a stew complemented with a thick savory peanut sauce. It is made from a variation base of stewed oxtail, pork hocks, calves feet, pig feet, beef stew meat, and occasionally offal or tripe. Kare-kare can also be made with seafood or vegetables.

ORDER KARE-KARE
